## 🛠️ 1. Core Copywriting Frameworks

### A. PAS (Problem, Agitation, Solution)
*   **Problem:** Define a specific, painful problem the audience is facing.
*   **Agitation:** Twist the knife. Explain the consequences of not solving this problem. Emotionalize the pain.
*   **Solution:** Present your knowledge/product as the only logical relief.
*   *Best for:* Social media ads, Reddit posts, Short-form video scripts.

### B. AIDA (Attention, Interest, Desire, Action)
*   **Attention:** A massive, scroll-stopping hook.
*   **Interest:** Fresh data, unique insights, or a counter-intuitive fact.
*   **Desire:** Show the "Dream State". How much better is life after knowing this?
*   **Action:** A clear, low-friction next step (Link click, Follow, Reply).
*   *Best for:* Blog posts, Email marketing, Long-form X threads.

### C. BAB (Before-After-Bridge)
*   **Before:** The world is messy, slow, or broken.
*   **After:** The world is perfect, fast, and automated.
*   **Bridge:** Reveal the "Secret Sauce" (The topic at hand) that makes the transition possible.
*   *Best for:* Case studies, Implementation guides.

### D. 4 U's (Useful, Urgent, Unique, Ultra-specific)
*   Every headline or hook must satisfy at least 2 of these.
*   *Best for:* Subject lines, First lines of threads.

## 🧠 2. Psychological Triggers (The 6 Principles)

1.  **Authority:** Use technical jargon, specific numbers, and reference credible sources (Arxiv, GitHub).
2.  **Scarcity:** "Only 1% of developers know...", "This window is closing...".
3.  **Social Proof:** Mention community buzz, Reddit sentiment, or GitHub stars.
4.  **Curiosity Gap:** State the result, but hide the method until the middle of the content.
5.  **Reciprocity:** Give away "too much value" for free to build trust.
6.  **Liking:** Use the "Persona's Voice" to build a relatable human connection.

## 🔥 3. 2024 Viral Hook Patterns

*   **The "Enemy" Switch:** "The industry says X, but the data proves Y."
*   **The Negative Call-Out:** "Stop doing X if you want Y."
*   **The "Illegal" Frame:** "This technique feels like a cheat code."
*   **The Experience Proxy:** "I analyzed 500 pages of documentation so you don't have to."

---

## 🛑 4. Universal Mandates
*   **No Fluff:** If a sentence doesn't add value or drive the framework forward, delete it.
*   **Mouthfeel:** Content must feel like it's coming from a tactical necessity, not an AI template.
*   **Evidence-Based:** Every claim must be grounded in the provided context.
